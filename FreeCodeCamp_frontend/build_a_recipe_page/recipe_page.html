"How to make an Adobo"

A simple guide for cooking adobo. This dish fits quick meals for busy days.

You get tender meat, balanced salt, sour taste, and steady aroma from common ingredients.

Ingredients for cooking: sliced pork, soy sauce bottle, vinegar cup, garlic cloves, bay leaves, and peppercorns arranged on a clean kitchen counter.

Ingredients

  • 1 kilogram pork belly, cut into cubes
  • 1 cup soy sauce
  • 1 cup vinegar
  • 1 head garlic, crushed
  • 1 teaspoon whole peppercorns
  • 3 bay leaves
  • 1 cup water

Instructions

  1. Preheat your pot on medium heat.
  2. Add pork and let the fat render until the edges turn brown.
  3. Pour soy sauce and vinegar. Do not stir.
  4. Add garlic, peppercorns, bay leaves, and water.
  5. Cover the pot and simmer for 40 minutes.
  6. Taste the sauce and adjust the salt level based on your preference.
  7. Simmer for 10 more minutes until the sauce becomes thick
  8. Rest the dish for a few minutes before serving.
